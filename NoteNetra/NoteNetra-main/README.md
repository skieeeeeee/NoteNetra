> **NoteNetra** is an affordable IIoT + Web system that transforms India’s offline cash-based shops into digitally visible, creditworthy businesses. It records every cash and UPI transaction, auto-generates business analytics, and creates a credit score — even for those without GST or CIBIL history.

---

## 🚀 Problem We’re Solving

Most of Delhi’s MSMEs operate in the shadows — no credit history, no GST, no access to growth tools.  
They are:
- Undiscovered by banks and big buyers
- Unable to join ONDC, eCommerce, or loan programs
- Lacking digital proof of business performance

🔍 **NoteNetra changes that.**

---

## 💡 Our Solution

### ⚙️ Hardware + Web Companion
- IIoT sensor device detects **offline cash payments**
- Web dashboard tracks **all transactions (cash + digital)** in real time

### 💳 Alternative Credit Score
- Creates a **NoteNetra Credit Score** from real cashflow
- Lets MSMEs **apply for loans** via pre-filled forms (RazorpayX, Indifi, Lendingkart)

### 📊 Business Dashboard
- Daily/monthly sales summary
- Top-selling items, buyer insights, stock alerts
- One-click GST-ready invoice download

### 🛒 Storefront Builder
- Generates QR-based **mini-store link** (e.g., notenetra.in/m/rajtools)
- ONDC plugin-ready for future integration

### 📚 MSME Growth Hub
- Short videos (Hindi + English) on:
  - How to take loans without CIBIL
  - How to use WhatsApp for business
  - Selling on ONDC
- Earn badges + certifications
